About

The Vllasaliu Lab focuses on understanding and overcoming biological barriers to improve drug delivery. Our overarching aim is to enable injection-free administration of biotherapeutics including peptides, proteins, and nucleic acids.

We work at the interface of nanomedicine, extracellular vesicles, intestinal biology, and translational drug delivery science to improve patient access to medicines.

Our research is supported by UKRI councils (BBSRC, EPSRC, Innovate UK, Research England), charities, and pharmaceutical industry collaborations.

Research Themes

  • Extracellular Vesicles and Exosome Engineering
  • Nanomedicine and Lipid-Based Drug Delivery Systems
  • Oral and Injection-Free Delivery of Biotherapeutics
  • Drug Delivery for Gut-Related Diseases (IBD)
  • Advanced In Vitro Models Including Organoids
